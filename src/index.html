About CyberSec 25

The third cybersecurity conference organized by the cybersecurity Master's students will be taking place on January 2023. For the first time this conference will be presential and will took place in the Escola Superior de Tecnologia e Gestão main auditorium. This event will connect anyone with a particular interest in cybersecurity with the students and the expert faculty. The latest cybersecurity trends and the emerging threats will be discussed along with the presentations. Conference registration is free but mandatory to attend.
